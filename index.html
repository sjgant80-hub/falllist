How sending works (BYOM)

FallList doesn't run an SMTP server. Instead, you bring your own:

  • Resend · easiest · resend.com · 3,000 free/mo then $20/mo
  • Postmark · best deliverability · ~$15/mo for 10,000 emails
  • Mailgun · cheap at scale · $1 per 1,000 emails
  • SendGrid · enterprise · 100/day free

Paste your chosen API key in Settings. FallList sends direct from your browser to the mailer's API · no server in the middle · no rate cuts.
